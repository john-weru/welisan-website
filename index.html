FAQ

Quick answers before you book.

Have specific requirements? Send them in the quote request and we’ll advise the best approach.

How do you determine CCTV camera placement?
We consider entry points, blind spots, lighting conditions, distance to subjects, and the most valuable angles for identification. We also plan power/network routing to keep the installation neat and reliable.
Can you build a website that works well on mobile?
Yes-our builds are mobile-first, optimized for speed, clear typography, and easy navigation. We can also integrate contact forms, WhatsApp/email, and basic SEO structure.
Do car trackers drain the battery?
When installed correctly, trackers are designed for low power consumption. We test the setup, wiring, and reporting to ensure stable operation and advise best practices if a vehicle is parked long-term.
What information do you need for a quote?
Service type, location, number of cameras/vehicles (if applicable), and any preferences (e.g., remote viewing, storage retention, pages required for a website). If unsure, we’ll guide you.
